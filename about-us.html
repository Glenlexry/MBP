Introduction to the firm

The ﬁrm of Messrs. Murali B. Pillai And Associates (“the Firm”) was established on 16th November 1990 with Mr. Murali B. Pillai as the sole-proprietor. On 6th September 1999 Ms. Amudha Jayaraman was appointed as a partner of the ﬁrm and on 1st September 2002, Mr. Sreether Sundaram joined the ﬁrm as a partner. Encik Mohd Taufek Bin Md Sani joined the ﬁrm as a partner in the month of February 2013 and Ms. Chrishanthini A.R. Sebastiampillai was appointed as a partner of the ﬁrm on 1st January 2014.


The ofﬁce of Messrs. Murali B. Pillai And Associates is situated at Suites 1504/1505, 15th Floor, Wisma Lim Foo Yong, No. 86, Jalan Raja Chulan, 50200 Kuala Lumpur.


As at the date of the Proﬁle there are 7 legal assistants, and 12 support staff employed by the ﬁrm.
